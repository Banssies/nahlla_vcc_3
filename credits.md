## 🌿 Inspiration

Aether draws inspiration from:

- the complexity of human emotion
- the quiet difficulty of starting when overwhelmed
- the idea that support should feel safe
- the belief that technology can be compassionate
- the need for systems that guide without shaming

It was shaped by the hope that even small, thoughtful prompts can create real change.

---

## ✨ Project Spirit

Aether exists in the space between:

- logic and feeling
- function and care
- structure and softness
- technology and humanity

Its foundation is not just code.  
It is philosophy.
